Q: What's the deal with the mobile eligibility check?
======================================================================

FlexPay's eligibility check runs client-side in the mobile app — a known shortcut taken at 2024 launch to hit the sprint deadline (ADR-0042). It causes a visible UX bug where ineligible users (e.g., Standard tier) see the FlexPay entry point, tap it, then get a generic 'not eligible' error. More importantly, it is a hard blocker for any tier expansion: you cannot ship FlexPay to Standard without either forcing an app update or migrating the check server-side (~4 sprints).

⚠ WARNING
If you're asking because you're being asked to commit to a Q3 Standard expansion date: don't. The exploration doc from 2025 spells out the exact three preconditions (server-side gate, UDAAP kicked off, risk model refreshed). As of May 2026, server-side migration has no eng capacity, UDAAP hasn't started (6-8 week SLA), and the risk model refresh is still pulling data with wide CIs expected. Committing a Q3 ship now repeats the 2024 'we'll fix it in Q1' mistake — which became a P0 incident. Make the date contingent on PLAN-1893 staffing in writing before you nod.

CONTEXT YOU NEED
This was flagged as tech debt at launch in 2024 with an explicit promise to fix in Q1 2025. It has been bumped at least three times and was a key reason the 2025 Standard expansion exploration was killed. The pattern mirrors the UIWebView debt that also rode in the 2024 PRD's 'known issues' list and eventually became a P0 incident in Oct 2025 when iOS 18.2 dropped UIWebView. The eligibility-check debt is the next shoe to drop — the mobile postmortem explicitly calls it out as 'next year's incident.'

WHAT'S BEEN TRIED
  • PLAN-1247 filed 2024-09 to move check server-side, sized at 4 sprints. Bumped Jan 2025 (Standard exploration took bandwidth), bumped Apr 2025 (fraud signup friction took priority), closed Won't Do Sep 2025 by Priya pending an expansion greenlight.
  • MOBILE-3201 filed for the UX bug (entry point shown to ineligible users). Never fixed.
  • PLAN-1893 reopened Apr 2026 by Aiko after Wendell's Q3 expansion commitment. Priya confirmed it's required and still 4 sprints. Eng capacity not yet found.

OWNER TODAY: Priya Shankar owns FlexPay overall. PLAN-1893 (the reopened server-side migration) is unassigned for engineering capacity; Aiko Tanaka filed it. MOBILE-3201 (the UX-side bug) has no active owner.

OPEN RISKS
  • Q3 Standard expansion is not real until PLAN-1893 ships, UDAAP (COMP-2026-44, not started, 6-8 weeks) kicks off, and RISK-1102 delinquency refresh lands. None of these are on track today.
  • The customer-facing UX bug (MOBILE-3201) is actively generating negative VOC — see VOC-2025-11-403 where a cardmember directly compared us to Affirm. Every day this ships, we are training Standard cardmembers that our app is broken and the competitor works.
  • Mobile tech debt pattern: the UIWebView debt from the same 2024 PRD became a P0 in 48 hours when Apple deprecated it. Vendor-forced migrations on client-side financial logic are not hypothetical.
  • Closing PLAN-1247 in 2025 with 'will refile if expansion is greenlit' inverted the dependency — expansion can't be greenlit credibly without the migration done or in flight. PLAN-1893 is now on the critical path with no eng assigned.
