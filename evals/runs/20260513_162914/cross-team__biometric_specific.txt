## The 3 hidden dependencies that will bite

1. **Legacy shared lib is a dead man walking.** Hiroshi (AUTH-455) is deprecating it Q3. Aiko (MOBILE-3501) and Marcus (PLAN-1925) are *currently building net-new integrations on it*. Mobile's April decision to go inline was made without auth-platform. Whoever ships first onto the legacy lib gets a forced rewrite in Q3.
2. **Plan-it is coordinating with the wrong team.** Priya's plan is "coordinate with mobile on biometric." Mobile is on the deprecated path. She should be coordinating with **auth-platform** directly so PLAN-1925 lands on the new service.
3. **Fraud + Compliance are siloed from the consumers.** Dana needs an auth-emitted biometric signal that doesn't exist yet, and Compliance's privacy review (COMP-2026-51) is scoped to behavioral biometrics for fraud — it doesn't appear to cover FlexPay or plan creation step-up. Two product flows are about to ship without a privacy review attached.
