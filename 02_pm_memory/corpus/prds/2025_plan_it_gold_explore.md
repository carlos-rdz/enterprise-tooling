# Exploration Doc: FlexPay Standard Expansion (KILLED)
**Status:** Killed 2025-Q3
**Author:** S. Kowalski

## Why explored
Premier activation hit target (11% vs 8% goal). Natural question: expand to Standard.

## Why killed
1. Standard cardmember segment skews younger, thinner credit files. Risk modeling showed loss rate ~2.4x Premier at same plan terms.
2. Servicing volume projection added 14 FTE — no budget approved.
3. The mobile client-side eligibility check (see 2024 PRD known issues) means any tier expansion requires either a forced app update OR moving the check server-side. Server-side migration was estimated at 4 sprints and got bumped twice.
4. UDAAP review for new segment estimated 6-8 weeks. Was not started.

## Lesson recorded
Any future expansion conversation must START with: (a) is the mobile gate server-side yet, (b) is UDAAP kicked off, (c) is risk modeling current. Without all three, Q+1 ship is not real.
