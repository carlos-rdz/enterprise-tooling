This exact expansion was explored and killed in 2025-Q3 for the same three reasons plus servicing FTE. Nothing structural has changed since then except a leadership commitment from Wendell.

⚠ WARNING
You are about to commit to a Q3 ship on a program that was killed less than a year ago for reasons that are still 100% true today. The killed-exploration doc was written specifically to prevent this conversation from happening without preconditions in motion — read it before your next meeting with Wendell. If you say yes to Q3 without server-side eligibility, UDAAP kickoff, and a current risk model all started this week, you are signing up to either (a) miss the date publicly, (b) ship to a forced-app-update-only population, or (c) ship the known broken UX (MOBILE-3201) to your most price-sensitive segment and create a servicing and compliance incident. Push back on the date or scope it to a precondition-completion milestone, not a ship milestone.

CONTEXT YOU NEED
FlexPay launched 2024-Q2 as Premier-only with a client-side eligibility check explicitly logged as tech debt (ADR-0042). That tech debt has now bitten twice: it blocked the 2025 Standard expansion exploration, and a related shortcut (UIWebView in the same flow) became a P0 crash incident in Oct 2025 when iOS 18.2 dropped support. The 2025 killed-exploration doc explicitly lists the preconditions for any future expansion conversation — you are now in that conversation and the preconditions are still not met. Separately, FlexPay is currently functioning as a Premier retention driver (VOC-2026-01-77: 'this is why I keep Premier') — expanding to Standard has a strategic cost beyond the operational one.

  • 2025-Q3 Standard expansion exploration (S. Kowalski): killed for (a) 2.4x loss rate at same plan terms, (b) 14 FTE servicing gap unfunded, (c) client-side eligibility gate blocks tier expansion without forced app update, (d) UDAAP review not started.
