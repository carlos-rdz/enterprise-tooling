You are about to walk into the exact trap that killed this expansion in 2025-Q3. The departing PM wrote the playbook for not repeating it — three preconditions, all currently unmet. If you accept a Q3 date this week without (a) eng capacity assigned to PLAN-1893, (b) Brett's UDAAP review kicked off, and (c) Dana's risk read in hand, you are signing up to either slip publicly, ship with a forced app update and crash-prone webviews, or ship at a loss rate outside the approved corridor. Push back on the date before you negotiate scope.

CONTEXT YOU NEED
FlexPay launched 2024-Q2 as Premier-only with explicit out-of-scope language for Standard/Green. Activation beat goal (11% vs 8%), which keeps generating pressure to expand. An exploration to expand to Standard was killed in 2025-Q3 by S. Kowalski (since departed) citing 2.4x loss rate on Standard at same terms, +14 FTE servicing with no budget, the mobile client-side eligibility gate, and an unstarted UDAAP review.
