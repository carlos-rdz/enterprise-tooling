## The 2-3 critical hidden dependencies

1. **Legacy lib death march.** Hiroshi is deprecating the exact library Aiko (mobile) just built MOBILE-3501 on top of, and that PLAN-1925 will almost certainly inherit. Both downstream tickets are "In Progress" against a soon-to-be-dead API. Nobody has socialized a migration path.
2. **Three teams independently building "biometric step-up"** (mobile FlexPay, plan-it >$1000, fraud step-up v2) with no shared contract. You're going to end up with three different UX patterns and three different signal payloads.
3. **Fraud needs a signal auth hasn't committed to expose.** FRAUD-1120 is blocked on auth emitting biometric outcomes for risk scoring — conversation is informal, no ticket dependency filed.
